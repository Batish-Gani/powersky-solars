FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a residential solar system cost?

Costs vary by system size, roof type, and components. Most homes choose 3kW to 8kW packages.

How long does installation take?

Typical residential installations are completed within 1 to 3 days after approvals.

What maintenance is required?

Solar panels need minimal maintenance, mainly periodic cleaning and annual performance checks.

Can I get subsidy support?

Yes. Our team guides you through eligible government subsidy and net metering documentation.
